Property Analysis

The state of the housing market has a significant impact on consumer decisions when it comes to purchasing a home. Analyzing housing market trends can provide valuable insights into the overall landscape, particularly in a state as diverse and dynamic as Florida. The analysis of housing market trends involves studying various indicators. This can include examining home prices, the rate of home sales, among other factors.

Analysis of the Deviation of Sales Price from Appraisal Value

select county and time period

This query represents a portion of the housing market's state. A period in which residential properties are sold for less than their market value denotes a weak market, whereas prices several orders of magnitude higher than their market value denotes a strong housing market. This analysis gives an idea of what is going on in order to make a decision to buy a home during that time period. For a given county, this query involves the quotient of the sale price by the market value subtracted by one. In this manner, the user can see monthly trends with each percentage (e.g., the sale price is 33% higher than the market value for month x.)

Analysis of the Influence of Age on Market Value

select county and time period

This query can provide valuable insights for several compelling reasons. For example, if a buyer prefers to buy an older property over a new one, they can use this information to gain negotiating power if older homes tend to appreciate at a slower rate. It can even help owners of older properties with highlighting their property’s value relative to its age. Both parties in such negotiations may benefit from the information revealed by this query.

The results of this query provide significant guidance to stakeholders.
