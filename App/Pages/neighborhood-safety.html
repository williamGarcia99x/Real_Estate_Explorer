Neighborhood Safety Analysis

On the Neighborhood Safety Analysis page, users can gain valuable insights into the impact of criminal activity on residential property sales prices in different counties across Florida. This feature is designed to cater to users who are not only interested in the aesthetics and amenities of a neighborhood but also want to assess the safety and security aspects. The analysis considers criminal offenses ranging from first to third degrees, allowing users to understand the varying degrees of severity in each county. This nuanced approach helps in providing a comprehensive view of the safety landscape.

Average Sale Prices Over Time

In this query, we want to look into the impact that criminal activity may have on residential property sales prices. Criminal offenses can range from first to third degrees in each county, depending on the severity of the crime. The query investigates whether severe or less severe average degrees of criminal offenses may lower or increase the value of a residential property in different counties over time. To determine a trend, we look at the most common degree of a criminal offense and the corresponding average sale price of a property in a county over time and compare it to other counties.
